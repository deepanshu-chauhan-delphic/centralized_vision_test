COMMERCIAL OFFICE

Leverage Advanced Technology of Real-Time, Virtual Monitoring

Whether a commercial property is rated as Class A, B or C office space, the integration of live remote monitoring can elevate an existing surveillance system, making it a valuable asset in maintaining a safe and productive work environment. Whether a single-tenant, multi-level office building with subterranean underground parking or detached garage, round-the-clock LIVE monitoring presents a cost-effective solution used to either compliment boots on the ground security personnel, replace it all together or even afterhours when tenants are no longer present. It goes without saying but immediate incident response capabilities can have a deterrent effect on criminal activity.

Virtual Guard, Greeter and Engineer contribute to the overall security and efficiency of busy commercial office environments as tenants, vendors and contract professionals come and go—during and after business hours. A custom “eye in the sky” solution for commercial office space with Smart Systems such as thermostat, lighting, flood detection, EMS, could also include Virtual Engineer to monitor those building systems and react to alerts in real time.

Key services available to monitor Class A, B and C office spaces include the following:

  • Vendor management with Virtual Greeter, commonly referred to as Virtual Reception in these environments available for building visitors, it safeguards everyone—from landscapers to cleaners, to delivery drivers— they’ll come to depend on CV to greet them, VET them and authorize them access to their destination.
  • Site and perimeter area monitoring (includes vacant space) detects and challenges vagrants, trespassers, illegal dumping, as well as ensures vehicle gates and doors appear to be operating and secured as expected. Individuals are addressed and challenged with audio talk down (if avail) and or security patrol or police, depending on the situation.
  • Smart Systems Oversight with Virtual Engineer, EMS, Thermostat, ISP, lighting, power, etc. are monitored with close attention to mitigate risk and prevent catastrophic events before they reach critical mass.
  • Visual Escort and Scout Ahead. Centralized Vision’s Surveillance Specialists are trained and “on call” for office personnel, round-the-clock, to request to be escorted while walking to and from their destinations. With Scout Ahead, callers may request an “all clear” prior to departure or arrival at their destination.
  • Card Access Administration and Oversight, whether acting as the administrator facilitating card activations or deactivations, or simply watching over various alert notifications to ensure all is safe and secure, Centralized Vision’s Surveillance Specialists are present round-the-clock and on the ready.
